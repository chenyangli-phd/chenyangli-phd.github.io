Available for work

Numerical Methods and Finite Element Analysis. My research is centered on the numerical analysis of partial differential equations arising in fluid dynamics and multi-physics systems, with a strong emphasis on finite element methods. In particular, I focus on stabilized mixed finite element formulations for incompressible flows. My work addresses key numerical issues such as stability, well-posedness and optimal error estimates, with special attention to time-dependent problems.

Variable-Density and Complex Fluid Models. I also work on time-dependent coupled models with variable density, where the loss of skew-symmetry and additional nonlinear transport effects pose significant analytical and numerical challenges.
